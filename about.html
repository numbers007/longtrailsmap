Welcome to LongTrailsMap.net: Interactive 3D maps of long distance nature trails from around the world!

The purposes of the site are:
  • To help members of the community find their next destination.
  • To increase awareness of the long trails, especially the newer and lesser known long trails.
  • To motivate the responsible development of new long trails, especially efforts to connect existing trails.

Disclaimer: This site is for entertainment purposes only, and should not be used for navigation. The paths shown on the map are intended to give the user an idea of the long trail hiking opportunities that are available, but some may be years out of date, may have reroutes not reflected on the map, may not have been recently maintained, or may have sections that are in the 'planning' stage, etc.
